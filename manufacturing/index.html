Where we make the difference

We don't touch your production systems. We work on the business support side — the parts that keep customers informed, invoices paid, and your team out of their inbox.

📋

Quotes & orders

From the moment an enquiry lands, your team should be selling — not entering data.

  • New enquiry received → contact created, quote triggered, follow-up reminder set
  • Quote accepted → job created in your system, customer sent an order acknowledgement
  • Unanswered quote → automatic chase sent before the lead goes cold
  • Order details updated → relevant team members notified immediately
💬

Customer communications

Customers want to know what's happening. Keeping them updated shouldn't depend on someone remembering to send an email.

  • Order confirmed → customer receives an automatic acknowledgement
  • Job reaches a key milestone → update sent to the customer without anyone lifting a finger
  • Job complete → confirmation sent, satisfaction follow-up scheduled
  • Query received → acknowledgement sent instantly, routed to the right person
🧾

Invoicing & accounts

Completed jobs should trigger invoices automatically. Payment chasing shouldn't fall to whoever remembers.

  • Job marked complete → invoice raised and sent to the customer automatically
  • Invoice unpaid after your chosen timeframe → polite payment reminder sent
  • Payment received → accounts record updated, team notified in one step
  • Month end → revenue summary sent directly to your inbox
🔗

Supplier & purchasing admin

Supplier comms and purchase orders generate a lot of back-and-forth. Most of it can be handled automatically.

  • PO raised → supplier notified automatically with the right details
  • Expected delivery date → reminder logged, team alerted if it's approaching
  • New supplier onboarded → details synced across your systems in one step
  • Price or lead time change → flagged to the relevant person straight away
