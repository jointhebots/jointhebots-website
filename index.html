We build with whatever you've already got.

Most businesses are running some combination of tools that don't talk to each other. Below are a few typical examples of apps we work with but the list doesn't stop here. If it has an API, we can connect it.

Your CRM on autopilot

Pipedrive is only as good as the data going into it, and most teams are too busy selling to keep it clean. We build automations that do the admin for you.

Typical automations

  • New lead comes in via website, LinkedIn, or referral → contact created in Pipedrive automatically, deal added to the right pipeline stage
  • Deal moves stage → automatic follow-up email or WhatsApp message sent to the prospect
  • Deal marked as won → onboarding checklist triggered, invoice raised in Xero, Slack notification sent to the team
  • Stale deals flagged → owner gets a reminder before the lead goes cold
  • Meeting booked via Calendly → Pipedrive contact updated, prep notes added to the deal

Accounts that update themselves

Xero holds your financial data, but getting it there usually means someone copying numbers between systems. We connect Xero to the rest of your stack so the books stay accurate without the manual entry.

Typical automations

  • Deal won in Pipedrive → invoice drafted in Xero and sent to the client automatically
  • Invoice paid → CRM updated, onboarding workflow triggered, team notified in Slack
  • New supplier or client added to your CRM → contact created in Xero to match
  • Overdue invoice → automated payment reminder sent via email or WhatsApp
  • Monthly revenue data pulled from Xero → summary report sent to your inbox or Slack

Meet your clients where they already are

WhatsApp has the open rates email only dreams about. We help businesses use it professionally, automated where it makes sense, personal where it matters.

Typical automations

  • New enquiry received → instant WhatsApp message sent to the lead while you're still top of mind
  • Booking confirmed → WhatsApp reminder sent 24 hours before the appointment
  • Deal progresses → client gets a quick update message at each key milestone
  • Support request logged → client receives a WhatsApp acknowledgement with a reference number
  • Invoice sent → friendly payment reminder via WhatsApp if unpaid after a set number of days

The right people, informed at the right time

Slack becomes genuinely useful when it surfaces things your team actually needs to know, not just another place to check. We wire it into your workflows so key updates arrive without anyone having to send them.

Typical automations

  • New deal created or won → instant Slack notification to the relevant channel or person
  • Lead response time tracked → alert fired if a prospect hasn't been followed up within your target window
  • Daily or weekly digest → revenue, pipeline, and task summaries posted to a Slack channel each morning
  • Automation error detected → immediate Slack alert so nothing breaks silently
  • Client onboarding milestone hit → Slack message to the team with next steps

The engine behind everything

n8n is the automation platform we build on. It runs securely on your own infrastructure, which means your data stays within your control and never passes through a third-party cloud. For businesses handling sensitive client information or working under GDPR, that matters.

What that means in practice

  • Multi-step workflows that span several apps in a single automated sequence
  • Conditional logic, so automations behave differently depending on the data
  • Self-hosted on your own server or cloud, so sensitive business data never leaves your environment
  • Full data sovereignty for businesses with compliance or privacy requirements
  • Error handling and alerts, so you know immediately if something goes wrong
  • A visual workflow editor so everyone on your team can see exactly how your automations work, not just the person who built them
